Contract

  • Standard record not sufficient
  • Custom contract with 3 managing directors
  • Draft contact by Notary
  • Many corrections (typos, misspelling, etc.)
  • Severability clause
- Standard record available - up to 3 shareholders/members - only one managing director - own contract necessary - no lawyer necessary, notary is fully trained jurist - Many corrections via emails + telefon - Severability clause: if one part/paragraph is invalid, the rest stays intact - Regulations for - Children / heirs - Marriage - Transfer of shares - bankruptcy

Contract signing

  • Hand-written supplements to the contract
  • Read aloud by notary
  • Signatures
- Appointment at notary's (17.11.2017) - Hand-written supplements - last few mistakes - filling in variables and placeholders - 3-4 signatures per person - fair copy by notary - XML transport to local court after payment

Opening a bank account

  • Articles of association necessary
  • Access rights for 3 of us
  • One week of processing time
  • More signatures

Deposit money!

- Evidence of share capital necessary - Checked by notary - "Cash deposit" into bank account - Chicken-egg problem: Company needs account <-> account demands company - Solution: opening account with articles of association - Access rights - normally only with 2 out of 3 people - in our case: individual authorisation -> everyone can at all times access the account - "only" one week waiting time (2 dates) - 10 signatures per person for: - opneing the account - individual authorisation * 3 - ATM cards

UG in Gründung

  • Having legal capacity since signing the contract
  • Commercial register entry
  • Business registration
  • Wait!
- exists since signing the contract - status "in Gründung (i.G.)" - able to sign contract in name of the company - no liability by company - commercial register entry necessary - business registration at city - long waiting time: - about 2 months local court of Cologne - about 2 weeks for registration

  • Requirement for sales tax ID
